Pune Smart City Dataset (R1,R2,R3,R4) - CC0 Public Domain

Subset of the Smart City Testbed collected by Pune Smart City Development Corporation Limited and IISC. This data can be used to predict the air quality index; given the data and parameters for a particular area (e.g railway station) in Pune, create a model to predict air quality index for other areas (e.g bus stops)

Smart City Index Headers(R1,R2,R3,R4) - CC0 Public Domain

Dataset containing information with some indexes about various cities from around the world. Some of the indexes are related to the government, environment and economy

Both datasets contain relevant information about smart cities. One refers to specific cities, the other one to specific places (railway stations, squares...)
