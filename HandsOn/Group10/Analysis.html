RESOURCE NAMING STRATEGY

For our resource name strategy we have decided to use hash URIs for our ontology, since this is relatively small, and slash URIs for the resources because we have a huge dataset.

Domain: “http://www.group10.org/”

Path: “http://www.group10.org/pl/”

Ontology: “http://www.group10.org/pl/ontology/”

Classes: “http://www.group10.org/pl/ontology/ParkingLotsMadrid#[className]”

Properties: “http://www.group10.org/pl/ontology/ParkingLotsMadrid#[propertyName]”

Individual: “http://www.group10.org/pl/resource/[className]/[individualIdentifier]”
