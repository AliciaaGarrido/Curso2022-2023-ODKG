APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS

The application allows users to locate on a map the different commemorative plaques in Madrid. By hovering over each plaque icon, a window with general information about the relevant historical person will be displayed.

In addition, links to pages with more important information about the historical person will be included.

The application will have a search engine that will allow filtering the search of the commemorative plaques based on keywords and entities that allow linking the data.

The app will also show the image of the correspondent commemorative plaque

This application will require data about:
- Commemorative plaques in Madrid
- Historical characters
