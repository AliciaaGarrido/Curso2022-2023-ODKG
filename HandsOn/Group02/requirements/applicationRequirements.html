Application Requirements

Dataset

The dataset is about traffic accidents in the City of Madrid registered by the Municipal Police, where at least one bicycle is involved.

Interface

The user can search for an accident or a set of accidents that have occurred thanks to several factors: the date, the location of the accident (street or neighbourhood), the type of accident in question, the age group, the condition of the person (drunk/drugged or not).

Visualization of the result

The result of the search can be visualised in the form of a list or on a map indicating the location of accidents. The user will be able to access other details on the accidents of his research contained in the data.
